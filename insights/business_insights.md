# Business Insights — Food Delivery Analysis

## Funnel Analysis

* Users Browsed: 5000
* Users Searched: 4990
* Users Checkout: 4358
* Users Ordered: 4985

### Key Insight

* Major drop observed at **Search → Checkout stage (~642 users)**
* Minimal drop in other stages

### Data Observation

* Orders exceed checkout users → possible tracking issue or direct ordering behavior

### Recommendation

* Improve menu UX and recommendations
* Highlight popular items and discounts during search

---

## Conversion Behavior

* Avg time to order: **20 days**
* Median time: **13 days**
* Avg sessions before order: **4**

### Insight

* Users require multiple touchpoints → not an impulse purchase

### Recommendation

* Retarget users within first 7 days
* Offer incentives during early sessions

---

## Engagement

* DAU stable between **470–580 users**

### Insight

* Product is stable but not growing significantly

### Recommendation

* Focus on acquisition campaigns

---

## Power Users

* Power users: **1057 (~20%)**

### Insight

* Highly engaged core user base

### Recommendation

* Introduce loyalty programs and subscriptions

---

## Revenue Trends

* Frequent revenue fluctuations observed

### Insight

* Revenue instability present

### Recommendation

* Analyze weekday vs weekend trends
* Introduce demand-balancing offers

---

## Average Order Value (AOV)

* Range: **₹750 – ₹900**
* No strong growth trend

### Insight

* Users are not increasing basket size

### Recommendation

* Bundle offers and upselling strategies

---

## Delivery Performance

* Avg delivery time: **44 minutes**

### Insight

* Slightly high delivery time may impact conversion

### Recommendation

* Optimize logistics and routing

---

## High Value Users

* Top users contribute significantly to revenue

### Insight

* Revenue concentration risk

### Recommendation

* Focus on retention of high-value users

---

## Retention Analysis

* Strong long-term retention observed across cohorts

### Observation

* Retention appears unusually flat → potential data bias

---

## Repeat Order Rate

* Repeat rate: **~98.8%**

### Critical Insight

* Unrealistically high → likely dataset bias

### Recommendation

* Validate data completeness before decision-making

---

# Final Summary

* Biggest issue: **Search → Checkout drop**
* Users need multiple sessions to convert
* Revenue is volatile but AOV is stable
* Strong power users but dependency risk exists
* Delivery time may be affecting conversion
* Data validation required for retention metrics

---

## Strategic Focus Areas

1. Improve conversion funnel
2. Optimize delivery time
3. Increase AOV
4. Retain high-value users
